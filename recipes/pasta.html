Tomato-Cream Sauce Pasta

Description

This cream sauce for pasta made with Italian-style diced tomatoes is absolutely delicious. It's rich, creamy, and so easy to make! Serve this foolproof sauce over your favorite pasta.

Ingredients

  • Onion and Garlic
  • Canned Tomatoes
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Heavy Cream
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Cook onions over medium-heat in olive oil until translucent, then stir in garlic.

  2. Add tomatoes, basil, sugar, oregano, salt, and pepper. Boil until the tomatoes have cooked down and most of the ingredients have evaporated.

  3. Remove the pan from heat, then stir in cream and butter. Return to the stove and simmer for about 5 minutes.

  4. Serve this tomato-cream sauce over your favorite pasta, such as penne or farfalle. For a heartier option, pour it over gnocchi or tortellini. You can shake things up a bit by adding meat (like chicken or sausage) and/or veggies (such as spinach or peppers).
